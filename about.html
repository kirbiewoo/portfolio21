about me

Hi, I am Kirby! I am a UX/UI designer that strive to unravel people problem and engage those whom I design for with empathy. I am passionate about creating meaningful relationships through technology and human-oriented design. Driven by care and resilience, I aspire to investigate, conceptualize, and create solutions that empower social changes with support and optimism.

Through working with like-minded teams and individuals, I hope to pursue a shared visiion where we push boundaries and bring diversity and perspectives into crafting intentional designs. My key strengths are in product design, user research, user experience design, stakeholder engagement, and selling ideas. When I am not designing, you can catch me walking my dog, JollyBean and going on food adventure!
